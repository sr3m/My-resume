About me

I'm an Electromechanical, Mechanical Engineer (and an ongoing Master of Science in Pure Mathematics) with management, CAD/CAE/CAM design, hydraulics, thermodynamic systems, failure analysis and mathematical modeling experience.

I have been working for the Engineering and Commissioning department of the LNG terminal expansion project at AES Andres. In this role, I have had the opportunity to contribute to the management and coordination of tests for all systems and equipment of the new LNG terminal. In addition, I have been involved in reporting on the progress of the commissioning process, analyzing and implementing solutions related to the first LNG terminal start-up (Purge and Dry-out, system cooldown and stabilization) and the stable-state regasification process, monitoring of operations, management and control of hazardous energy, and documentation control [including the constructive deviations correction management system (Oracle Unifier)].

I was a member of the teaching assistance staff at the "Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo" in the Mathematics School of the Faculty of Sciences, where I spent more than 10 hours per week in explaining the topics covered by math professors, performing exercises, clarifying doubts, and correcting assignments in various subjects. Also, I was a member of the coordination team with the main role of assigning and scheduling all the teaching assistance staff to all math subjects schedule by the University for each semester.

Additionally, I am a freelance graphic designer with extensive experience and a Python programmer.

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Pure Mathematical Sciences at the Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo, focusing in mathematical abstractions, functional and real analysis, measurement theory and statistics.
